### Our Vision

Ammit was founded on the belief that losing your device shouldn't mean losing your identity, and that true security should not come at the expense of usability. 

We are currently building the prototype of our core MPC wallet architecture and messaging framework. Once complete, we aim to test drive our solution with forward-thinking organizations to measure its impact on real-world identity security.
