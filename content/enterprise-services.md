### What We Offer

**Custom MPC Deployments**  
Integrate our threshold cryptography into your existing identity stacks to secure your employees and customers against internal and external threat vectors.

**Hosted Infrastructure**  
We provide highly-available, distributed computing clusters (co-signers) to act as the second party in the MPC protocol for your users, ensuring maximum uptime and preventing single-point key exposure.

Upgrade from legacy auth protocols. Seamlessly integrate advanced Passkey validation backed by MPC to protect against phishing and lost devices for your enterprise systems.
