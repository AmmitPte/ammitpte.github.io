Failsafe Design

Centralized systems allow for recovery should endpoint devices be lost or compromized. Decentralized systems improve privacy and cross-borders. Both have single points of failure. Can we produce systems with the privacy and cross-boundary abilities of decentralized system but without single points of failures — particularly endpoint devices?

Ammit aims to provide failsafe solutions. Using a model inspired by aviation design, we don't stop at trying to prevent intrustions, but assume that devices or services will be compromised. Using advanced cryptographic techniques such as MPC and threshold signatures, we ensure that when such a failure happens the damage done is minimal and clients are able to recover quickly and with little cost.
